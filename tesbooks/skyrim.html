Skyrim books in printable format

Here you can find all (well not yet) books found in TES V: Skyrim, in printable format.

I copied the contents of all books in Skyrim from the Fandom wiki into easily printable pdf files, the page size being A5. In the table below, you can see the title of every book, their file size and the download link. Also, there is a "download all" button above the table which, well, does exactly that.
